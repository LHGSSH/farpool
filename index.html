TRANSIT ISN'T JUST FOR CITY FOLK ANYMORE

Do you live in a rural or suburban area? Have you ever been stuck without a car? If so, you may have found it difficult to get to work or school. Public transit mostly only exists in urban areas, which is unfortunate for those who do not live in such places. FarPool is here to help! With FarPool, you can create and join carpools to wherever you need to go.

COVER YOUR COMMUTE

No more worrying about your car breaking down. Simply type in a destination, and we'll show you the carpools you can join. Pick the one that works best for you and sign up for a commute that you can trust.

HELP YOUR COMMUNITY

Don't see a carpool for your destination? Why not make one? It's easy to start a carpool on FarPool. Tell everyone where you'll be heading to and on what days and times. It's a great way to support your community!
