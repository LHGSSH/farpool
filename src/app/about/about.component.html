TRANSIT ISN'T JUST FOR CITY FOLK ANYMORE

Do you live in a rural or suburban area? Have you ever been stuck without a car? If so, you may have found it difficult to get to work or school. Public transit mostly only exists in urban areas, which is unfortunate for those who do not live in such places. FarPool is here to help! With FarPool, you can create and join carpools to wherever you need to go.

COVER YOUR COMMUTE

No more worrying about your car breaking down. Simply type in a destination, and we'll show you the carpools you can join. Pick the one that works best for you and sign up for a commute that you can trust.

HELP YOUR COMMUNITY

Don't see a carpool for your destination? Why not make one? It's easy to start a carpool on FarPool. Tell everyone where you'll be heading to and on what days and times. It's a great way to support your community!

ABOUT THE APP

FarPool was created out of a desire to create better public transit in America. The United States has fallen behind other countries when it comes to public transportation. While many countries, especially those in Europe, have reliable bus and train systems, the United States is dependent on the private car.

This is especially true for those living in rural or suburban areas. Very few of these areas have any public transit. Thus, those who cannot afford a car may be very limited in terms of job opportunity. Even in urban areas, U.S. public transit can be slow and unreliable.

The benefits of public transit are hard to ignore. It creates jobs, reduces carbon emissions, and lowers gasoline use. It can even help people escape poverty. These are reasons why public transportation in the U.S. should be improved. FarPool aims to make life in the US better through reliable public transit.

ABOUT THE CREATOR

Liam Hilton-Green and Nick Norris are students at the University of West Florida studying Software Design & Development. This website is a project for their Client-Side Programming class. Liam was inspired to make FarPool after seeing firsthand how difficult it can be to get a job when a person lives in a rural area without a car.
